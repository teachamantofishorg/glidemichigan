Weather pageÔÉÅ

This page specifically targets sites in the greater Traverse City area: Sleeping Bear Dunes, Greenpoint, Acadia, Empire, and Pyramid Point.

Ideal conditions: Around 20mpg @ 260. Primary weather resources:

  • Windy: Direction, speed, and forecast

  • NOAA Buoy: Offshore wind (removed in winter) reading several miles away

  • Elberta pier: Current wind

  • Elberta airport: Current wind

  • Water temperature: Cold water near launch can alter the prevailing wind direction when a cold water blob parks offshore.
